#ubuntu-indonesia is an Indonesian IRC community on freenode. Started as offtopic chatting place for ubuntu-indonesia.com forum users. Nowadays, it's an open place for everyone to learn, share, and discuss various topic including technology, security, gardening, daily news, memes, and various other things. Despite it's name, this place is open for any OS/Distro users.

Join

Join #ubuntu-indonesia on freenode using your favourite IRC client (i.e. HexChat), or follow links below:

  • ircs://chat.freenode.net/#ubuntu-indonesia
  • Kiwi IRC (Web Based IRC Client)
